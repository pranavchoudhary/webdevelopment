Our Menu

Chicken Biryani

Chicken pieces marinated with ginger, garlic, tumeric, cumin and chilli powder, slow cooked with basmati rice. Topped with ghee, fried onions, saffron, mint and coriander leaves. Served with raita and gravy.

Litti Chokha

A traditional dish form the state of Bihar. Wheat balls stuffed with roasted chickpea flour mixed with ginger, garlic and onions is baked on cow-dung cakes and dipped in ghee. Served with eggplant, potator and tomato chokha.

Aam Panna

A sweet sour and salty summer cooler made with young mangoes and spiced with roasted cumin, black salt, ginger and mint.


Subject to availability of young mangoes
